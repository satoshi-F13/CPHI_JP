## Data Limitations

### Not Available:
1. **C-level/Executive Numbers**: Not reported in official statistics
2. **Binding Contracts**: Not collected/published by organizers
3. **Historical Trends Pre-2023**: Limited public data availability
4. **Detailed 2025 Demographics**: Post-show report not yet fully released
5. **Exact Visitor Numbers by Country**: Only top 15 countries reported for 2024
6. **Regional Breakdown 2023 & 2025**: Only available for 2024

## Data Quality Notes

### High Confidence Data:
- Total exhibitor and visitor counts (official reports)
- Country representation numbers
- Top 15 country rankings
- Satisfaction percentages (from official surveys)

### Estimated/Calculated Data:
- Actual visitor counts by category (calculated from percentages)
- Some 2023 figures reported as "420+" and "20,000+" (minimum values)

## Data Usage Recommendations

1. **Year-over-year comparisons**: Use caution when comparing 2023 data due to limited detail
2. **Trend analysis**: Focus on 2024-2025 for most reliable trends
3. **Geographic analysis**: 2024 provides most complete picture
4. **Visitor analysis**: 2024 is the gold standard dataset
